01 · The problem

A diary can hold the entries. It can't hold the maths.

A small thekedar is running Kothrud, Baner, and Wakad at once — plus a flat interior in Kalyani Nagar. The record of all four lives in a paper diary, a WhatsApp group with the mistri, and your own memory.

Where today's way breaks

  • ✕ The diary has today's muster, but never today's total. You can't add up four sites in your head at 9pm.
  • ✕ Material bills come on WhatsApp and get lost above the next 200 messages.
  • ✕ Advances to workers are remembered, not recorded — until two people claim the same Rs 5,000.
  • ✕ You only learn a site lost money after the client's final payment clears. Too late to fix.

02 · The insight

Contractors don't lack entries. They lack the one number that a diary can never show: this site, right now, against the price I quoted.

Muster units × rate, plus material, plus advances — measured against the quoted budget, live. That is the whole wedge. Do that one sum well, on every site, every day, and the overspend can't hide.

03 · How it works

Log the day. Read the P&L. That's it.

  1. 1

    Open a book per site

    Add each site with its quoted budget — Kothrud Villa at Rs 12,50,000, Baner shop at Rs 6,80,000. Every entry after this rolls up under its site.

  2. 2

    Mark the daily muster

    Enter how many workers turned up and their rate. Muster units × rate becomes today's labour payout — no adding heads on your fingers at night.

  3. 3

    Add material & advances

    Snap a cement or ply bill in as a material amount. Record an advance to Suresh against his site. Both hit the running cost the second you save.

  4. 4

    Read the site P&L

    Running cost vs quoted budget, per site, instantly. Cross the budget and ThekaBook flags it before the next material order goes out.

FAQ

Do I have to change how I record the day?

No. You still note who came and what you bought — you just note it against a site. ThekaBook does the totalling and the P&L for you.

Will my mistri need to learn it?

No. You enter the muster and bills yourself, the same entries you already put in the diary. The mistri keeps sending photos on WhatsApp; you log them in seconds.

What if I only run two sites?

Then you'll see two site P&Ls instead of five. The value is the running-cost-vs-budget number, and that helps from your very first site.
